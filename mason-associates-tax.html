About the Firm

Three decades, two towns, one set of books.

Mason & Associates Tax Service got its start in Truckee, California — a place where the tax season overlaps a little too neatly with ski season. Glenn built his practice there working with year-round residents, seasonal business owners, and the occasional rental-cabin investor trying to make sense of depreciation schedules at 6,000 feet.

In 2012, the firm relocated its main office down the hill to Auburn, putting Glenn closer to a growing base of clients in Placer and Nevada counties while staying within easy reach of long-time Truckee families. He still keeps a presence up in Truckee, especially in the weeks leading up to filing deadlines, for clients who'd rather not make the drive.

Today, Glenn Steven Mason, EA continues to run the practice himself: same person preparing your return, same person you'll talk to if the IRS sends a letter.

Tax Season FAQ

Questions we hear every year.

Quick answers — if you don't see yours here, that's exactly what the consultation is for.

Bring last year's return, all W-2s and 1099s, mortgage and property tax statements, records of any estimated payments, and documentation for major life changes — a new home, a new business, marriage, or a dependent.
Individual returns are generally due April 15. We can file a six-month extension for you, but note that an extension to file is not an extension to pay — any tax owed is still due by the original deadline.
E-filed returns with direct deposit are typically processed within 2-3 weeks by the IRS. California refunds usually follow a similar timeline. Paper returns take significantly longer.
Both Enrolled Agents and CPAs have unlimited representation rights before the IRS. EAs are federally licensed specifically in tax matters and must complete 72 hours of continuing education every three years, focused entirely on taxation.
Most clients drop off or upload documents through the client portal and handle the rest by phone or email. In-person appointments are available at our Auburn office, with limited Truckee availability during tax season.
